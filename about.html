Our Story

Built on a Passion for Ethiopian Culture & Excellence

Akirma Events PLC was founded with a single vision: to bring world-class event management to Ethiopia while honoring the rich cultural heritage of the Habesha people. What began as a small team with big dreams has grown into the country's most trusted event planning company.

From our headquarters on Bole Road in Addis Ababa, we've organized events ranging from traditional Habesha weddings to large-scale government ceremonies attended by tens of thousands. Every event, large or small, receives the same dedication to excellence.

We operate nationwide — from Addis Ababa to Lalibela, Bahir Dar, Hawassa and beyond — bringing our team's expertise to every corner of Ethiopia.
